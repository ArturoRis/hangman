If you don't know what is the hangman game here a simple explanation
A group of people choose a "Master" that has to think about a word that the others have to guess.
The other players, each turn, ask the master if a letter is present in the word.
If the letter is present the master will write the letter in the word, everytime it occurs.
If the letter is not present the master will draw a part of a stick-man that is going to be hanged.
The game finished when a player guesses the word (and he wins) or when all the parts of the stick-man are drawn (the master wins).
